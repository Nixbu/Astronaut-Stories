What is this app?

This is a web application that allows you to explore Mars through the eyes of NASA's rovers. You can:

  • Search photos by Earth date
  • Filter images by different rovers and their cameras
  • Save your favorite photos
  • Create your own Mars story

How to use?

  1. Select a date to view available Mars photos
  2. Use filters to find specific rover photos
  3. Save interesting photos to your collection
  4. Create and share your Mars story
